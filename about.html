What is the DLC?

Our Objectives:

Become a Member Learn More


The objective of the Dawson Law Club is to create opportunities to learn about, become introduced to, and delve into the field of law. The goal of the club is not to provide legal education but rather to stimulate the interest a student may have — be it an interest in true crime or TV courtroom shows, to a supposed career path. The Dawson Law Club seeks to provide students with information and activities related to the field of law that extend beyond the classroom in an environment surrounded by peers. In its most basic form, the Dawson Law Club can help a student decide whether they want to pursue law or give students access to activities related to law. In its core, the club can be a place where interested students can learn, discuss, practice, and share their interests or passions about law.
